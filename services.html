Delivery & Packaging


Fresh to your door, every week.

How Delivery Works

Your ChefBox is packed fresh and delivered to your doorstep every Monday morning. All ingredients are vacuum-sealed and packed in insulated boxes to stay fresh for up to 5 days.

Delivery Coverage

  • Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore
  • Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai
  • More cities coming soon

Packaging

  • 100% recyclable materials
  • Insulated ice-pack liners
  • Clearly labelled meal bags
  • Zero single-use plastic
